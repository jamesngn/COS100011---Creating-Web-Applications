What are the best TVs to buy?
Finding the best TVs means working out what works best for you. Some TVs may be better for sport watchers, and other TVs may suit those who prefer movie or series. Some are cheap and some cost a bit more. Either way, at JB we’ve got a heap of different options including:
  • LED TVs.Light-emitting diodes result in a higher refresh rate, more contrast and brighter colours.
  • LCD TVs.Similar to LED, and also backlit, but uses a Liquid Crystal Display.
  • OLED TVs.An organic version of a LED. Light is created when a current passes through a carbon film.
  • QLED TVs.Uses quantum tech with the dots producing their own light. Huge colour.
  • QD-OLED.Mixing quantum dots with OLED tech means a brighter image and deeper contrast and blacks.
What are smart TVs?
Smart TVs are TVs that give you a heap of extra tech, extra features and an overall better viewing experience. A Smart TV allows you to:
  • Connect to the internet.Watch your favourite streaming apps, YouTube, or search the web.
  • Manage your smart home.Use your TV with your other Connected Home devices.
  • Buy a big brand.All the big brands have quality Smart TVs to choose from.
  • Connect your devices.Some Smart TVs have built-in Chromecast so you can link your phone, tablet or laptop.
